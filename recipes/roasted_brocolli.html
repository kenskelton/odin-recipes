Simple Roasted Broccoli

This roasted broccoli is crisp on the edges, tender in the center, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 large heads of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Optional: 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional: squeeze of fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Set oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Prepare broccoli: Wash and dry thoroughly. Spread florets evenly on a large baking sheet.
  3. Season: Drizzle with olive oil and toss with garlic, salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes. Spread into a single layer.
  4. Roast: Bake for 20–25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until edges are crispy and slightly browned.
  5. Finish: Remove from oven and top with Parmesan and a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.

Serving Size

Serves 4–6

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve alongside pasta, chicken, or steak
  • Add to grain bowls or salads
  • Toss with cooked pasta and extra Parmesan for a quick meal
