Classic Fudgy Brownies

These classic fudgy brownies are rich, chewy, and deeply chocolatey with a soft center and slightly crackly top.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional)
  • Optional: 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter and sugar. Add the eggs and vanilla, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Stir in the flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Fold in extras: Stir in chocolate chips and nuts if using.
  5. Bake: Spread the batter evenly in the prepared pan. Bake for 28-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  6. Cool and serve: Let brownies cool completely before cutting into squares for the cleanest slices.

Serving Size

Serves about 16–24 brownies, depending on how you cut them.
